About Our Pies

Ms. Margie's Sweet Potato Pies are made from an old Southern Family recipe that has been handed down through the generations. We use the freshest ingredients to make our pies special for you and your family.

Ms. Margie's offer three different sizes of pie.
From sizes big enough for the whole family, to individual sizes.

Enough for the whole family. Take it with you for those holiday gatherings, the family picnics, or as a special treat after dinner.

Our 5″ Sweet Potato is just enough for two, but there’s no guilt if you want to eat it all by yourself!

Try our 3" Sweet Potato Pie! Just enough for one person, but will leave you wanting for more!
